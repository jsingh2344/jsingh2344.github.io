A bit about myself

I climb, code, paint, and watch a lot of basketball and football. This website showcases portfolios for each (they often mix!). Recently, I've been spending about seven months a year at school in Pittsburgh, three working for the National Park Service in northwest Wyoming, and the remaining time at home in Washington, D.C.

My current interests/goals are:

  • Climbing longer alpine routes like the North Ridge of the Grand Teton, and linkups like the Grand Traverse
  • Working with geospatial data, particularly in machine learning/remote sensing applications
  • Painting some of the memorable experiences and landscapes from working and climbing in Grand Teton National Park
  • Watching Jayden Daniels lift the Lombardi trophy
