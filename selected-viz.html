When I was first getting into the NBA and NFL, I spent as much time watching games as I did playing around in RStudio with game data. A lot of these plots have to do either with direct curiosities I had or are focused on interesting statistical anomalies. The only extended investigation I did was into the relationship between the impacts of Derrick Henry and Ryan Tannehill, the offensive stars of the 2020 Tennessee Titans. I have a page dedicated entirely to that here.
